Sense360 Firmware Installer

Choose your hardware, install the right firmware in your browser, and connect the hub to Wi-Fi — in under 60 seconds.

Before you start: use desktop Chrome, Edge, or Opera with a USB data cable. Show details
  • Browser Use desktop Chrome, Edge, or Opera. Firefox, Safari, and mobile browsers don’t support Web Serial.
  • Cable A USB data cable (not charge-only) plugged directly into your computer. Avoid hubs and long extensions.
  • Other apps Close anything else using the serial port (Arduino IDE, PlatformIO, ESPHome dashboard).
  • After flashing Keep the tab open — we’ll guide you through Wi-Fi setup over the same serial connection (Improv).

If your hub isn’t detected when you click Install, hold BOOT, tap RESET, then release BOOT to enter recovery mode and try again. Most devices don’t need this step.

    Before you flash

    Review these essentials to keep your hub safe during the update.

    • Verify the Sense360 hub is powered and connected to your computer.
    • Keep this browser tab open until the flashing process finishes.
    • Do not disconnect power/USB during flashing.

    What happens next: after flashing, this tab will offer to connect your hub to Wi-Fi over the same USB connection (Improv Serial). Have your Wi-Fi password ready.

    Compatible Firmware
